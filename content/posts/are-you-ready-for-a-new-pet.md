Bringing a new animal home is one of the most rewarding decisions you can make, and also one of the biggest. A pet isn't just cuddles on the couch; it's daily feeding, vet visits, cleanup, training, and years of showing up even on the days you're tired or busy.

This quick quiz isn't a pass-or-fail test. It's a gentle mirror to help you reflect on your time, space, budget, and lifestyle before you commit. Answer 7 honest questions and you'll get a snapshot of how ready you are right now, plus a sensible next step whatever your score.
