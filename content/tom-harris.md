Tom Harris has kept and cared for exotic animals since childhood and has spent years studying reptile husbandry, avian nutrition, and small mammal health. He fills a real gap in pet content where most resources focus on cats and dogs while millions of households keep rabbits, parrots, geckos, and ferrets. At Pet Doctor Guide, he covers care guides for exotic and less common pets.
